Menu

redvelvet

A classic red velvet cake typically includes flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, oil, sugar-free sweetener, soy milk, vanilla extract, red food coloring, and a touch of vinegar. These ingredients come together to create the signature rich, moist texture and vibrant red color that make red velvet cakes so distinctive and delightful.

Brookie

A brookie, a delightful fusion of brownie and cookie, combines the best of both worlds. Its ingredients usually include flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, oil, sugar-free sweetener, chocolate chips, and sometimes vanilla extract or salt for flavor enhancement. These components result in a decadent treat with a chewy, fudgy texture and a perfect balance of chocolatey goodness.

tiramisu

Tiramisu is an italien dessert combines ladyfingers soaked in espresso with a creamy mixture of vegan mascarpone cheese, sugar-free sweetener. Optional additions include Marsala wine or rum, cocoa powder, dark chocolate, and whipped cream.

choclate cake

A simple chocolate cake usually contains flour, sugar-free sweetener, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, salt, soy milk, oil , and vanilla extract. Optional additions include hot water or coffee for richer flavor and frosting ingredients if desired.

Éclair

For vegan, sugar-free éclairs, use gluten-free flour, sugar-free sweetener, cocoa powder, plant-based milk, oil or vegan butter, vanilla extract, cornstarch, unsweetened almond or coconut milk, and sugar-free dark chocolate for the glaze. Adjust quantities as needed and follow traditional recipes with these substitutions.

cookies

Sugar-free vegan cookies cater to both dietary and health-conscious preferences, crafted from wholesome ingredients like almond and coconut flour, sweetened naturally with ingredients like applesauce or stevia. Whether it's classic chocolate chip or hearty oatmeal raisin, these treats offer guilt-free indulgence without compromising flavor.

strawberry cake

Strawberry cake captures the essence of ripe strawberries in a moist, fluffy texture. Enhanced with vanilla and almond flavors, it's a visually stunning treat perfect for any occasion. Whether dusted with powdered sugar or frosted with strawberry buttercream, each slice offers a delightful balance of tangy sweetness. Enjoyed as a snack or dessert, strawberry cake brings a taste of summertime joy in every bite.

Raspberry Charlotte Cake

Raspberry Charlotte Cake combines fresh raspberries with sponge cake or ladyfingers and a creamy mousse filling. Topped with a glaze or powdered sugar, it offers a delightful balance of sweet and tangy flavors, perfect for any special occasion.

cheesecake

Vegan sugar-free cheesecake offers a guilt-free twist on the classic dessert, made with a creamy blend of cashews, coconut milk, and coconut oil. Sweetened naturally and featuring a nut or date crust, it maintains its decadence without added sugars. Whether topped with fresh fruit or a chocolate drizzle, each slice delivers a rich and indulgent experience perfect for satisfying dessert cravings while aligning with vegan and sugar-conscious lifestyles.
